My Achievements

A journey of dedication, innovation, and continuous learning in Embedded Systems, IoT, Robotics, and Engineering.

Awarded as the Best Performer of the Batch (2024) during my Summer Training at Techpile Technology Pvt. Ltd., Lucknow. The training focused on PCB Design, IoT, and Embedded Systems. Recognized for outstanding technical performance, project execution, and dedication throughout the program.

🥇 Innovation Idea Essay Competition – BIS

Honored by the Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS) for excellence in the Innovation Idea Essay Writing Competition held at Government Polytechnic Hardoi. Recognized for presenting innovative technical ideas with practical implementation vision.

Earned the opportunity to visit the UP International Trade Fair, Noida after securing top rank in my college (Government Polytechnic Hardoi). This experience exposed me to industrial innovations, startup technologies, and emerging engineering solutions at a large-scale platform.
